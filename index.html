My Story

I'm a developer who genuinely enjoys the craft; not just writing code, but thinking about why something should be built in a certain way and making sure it works reliably when real people use it. I work at the intersection of AI and backend engineering, and I find that combination really works for me: intelligent systems are only as good as the infrastructure they run on.

AI is where my curiosity runs the deepest, understanding how language models work, making their use in real products, and building systems that go beyond demos. At the same time, I take backend development seriously as a discipline as it carries the weight of the entire system: clean API design, thoughtful data flow, and code that other people can actually work with.

Outside the tech world, I'm a big reader and someone who takes fitness pretty seriously. Hitting the gym and staying fit is less of a routine and more of a reset button for me. I think staying sharp physically and mentally makes for a better engineer, and honestly, a better human.
